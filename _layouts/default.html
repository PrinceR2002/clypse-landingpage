FAQ

Clear answers for the first-time visitor.

What can Clypse identify?

Clypse currently supports a growing set of movies and TV shows through surrounding audio, screen recordings, and spoken quotes, but it does not cover every title yet.

Does it support every movie and show?

No. The database is still expanding, and new titles are added every week, so coverage improves over time.

What happens after a match?

You get useful metadata like timestamp, year, ratings, genres, and providers, plus the option to save clips or quotes.

What if Clypse cannot identify something yet?

That usually means the title is not in the current database yet. Coverage is actively growing every week as more titles are added.

Is there more than recognition?

Yes. Clypse also lets you save recognized clips and quotes, review metadata, and play Framed and Quotele.
